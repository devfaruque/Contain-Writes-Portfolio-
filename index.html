The Vision Behind Lummar

Founded by architect Elena Vasquez in 2019, Lummar Studio emerged from a passion for creating lighting that transcends mere functionality. Each piece reflects years of architectural training and an unwavering commitment to precision.

Our philosophy centers on the belief that light shapes space, and space shapes experience. Every Lummar fixture is meticulously crafted to enhance architectural environments while maintaining the clean, minimalist aesthetic that defines contemporary design.

From our studio in Bucharest, we combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge LED technology, creating pieces that are both timeless and innovative.
